In the era of digital transformation, ensuring secure user authentication has become paramount to protect sensitive data and prevent unauthorized access. This paper presents an innovative E-Authentication System that integrates three advanced verification techniques:

1. Face recognition

2. One-Time Password (OTP) verification and

3. QR code verification.

The system employs face recognition technology to identify users through their unique facial features, offering a seamless and contactless method of authentication.

To further strengthen the authentication process, the system incorporates OTP verification, which generates a temporary code sent to the user's registered mobile device or email. This ensures that even if an attacker compromises a user's credentials, they cannot access the system without the OTP.

Additionally, QR code verification introduces a modern and efficient method for secure access. Users can quickly scan unique QR codes generated for their accounts, facilitating easy and rapid authentication.

By combining these three layers of security, the e-Authentication System offers a robust solution that significantly mitigates the risks associated with identity theft and unauthorized access. The multi-factor approach not only enhances security but also improves user experience, making it suitable for various applications, including online banking, e-commerce, and enterprise environments. This innovative system stands as a proactive measure against evolving cyber threats, fostering user trust and confidence in digital platforms.
